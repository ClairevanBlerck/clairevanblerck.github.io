Neem oil

Potentially toxic to childeren and pregnant persons if ingested

In addition to its use as an organic insecticide spray, this oil has been used medicinally and in the cosmetics industry. Neem oil and the tree from which it is derived are so called from the Sanskrit, nimba.

What

One seller of the product, Dyna-Gro, explains how it works as an organic insecticide: "It disrupts insects' hormonal balance so they die before they molt to the next life stage."

How

Mixing the oil with a premixed solution of warm soapy liquid is recommended because it helps the neem oil dissolve

  1. Mix 1 cup warm water with one pump of eco-friendly dishwashing liquid
  2. Add 1/3 teaspoon neem oil, shake, and use within 8 hours

Where

Your neem oil solution can be sprayed all over the plant leaves, as well as the soil.
